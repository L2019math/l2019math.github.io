左边积分是 ln(u+1+u2)ln(u+\sqrt{1+u^2})ln(u+1+u2​)

所以 $ y+\sqrt{x^2+y^2}=C$

y+x2+y2=12即  y=14−x2y+\sqrt{x^2+y^2}=\frac{1}{2} \\ 即 \,\,y=\frac{1}{4}-x^2 y+x2+y2​=21​即y=41​−x2
